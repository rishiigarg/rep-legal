Introduction

Diagnostic Data

Other Data

India Privacy Rights (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)

If you are located in India, you have the following rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to obtain a summary of your personal data being processed
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: You have the right to register a grievance with us regarding your personal data
  • Right to Nominate: You can nominate another individual to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity

We process your personal data only for lawful purposes and with your consent. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section above.
